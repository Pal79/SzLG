# Azonosság

---

$$
\begin{aligned}
(7+5x)2 &= (4x+5)3 && / zf \\
14+10x &= 12x+15 && / -14 \\
10x &= 12x+1 && / -12x \\
-2x &= 1 && / :(-2) \\
x = -\frac{1}{2} &= -0,5
\end{aligned}
$$

---

$$
\begin{aligned}
5x+9-7x+4 &= 8+2x-3(2x+6) && / zf \\
5x+9-7x+4 &= 8+2x-6x-18 && / öv \\
-2x+13 &= -10-4x && / +2x \\
13 &= -10-2x && / +10 \\
23 &= -2x && / :-2 \\
-\frac{23}{2} = -11,5 &= x
\end{aligned}
$$

---

$$
\begin{aligned}
3x+9-2(5x-6)(-3) &= 5x+4-4(x-2) && / zf \\
3x+9+6(5x-6) &= 5x+4-4x+8 && / zf \\
3x+9+30x-36 &= 5x+4-4x+8 && / öv \\
33x-27 &= x+12 && / -x \\
32x-27 &= 12 && / +27 \\
32x &= 39 && / :32 \\
x &= \frac{39}{32}
\end{aligned}
$$
