# Magasabb fokszámú egyenletek
## Tk.2177 feladat
### a. feladat
- $x^{4} - 5x^{2} + 4 = 0$
    - új ismeretlen bevezetése: $y = x^{2}$
- $y^{2} - 5y + 4 = 0$
    - $a = 1$
    - $b = -5$
    - $c = 4$
- $y1,2 = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^{2} - 4 \cdot a \cdot c}}{2 \cdot a} = \frac{5 \pm \sqrt{(-5)^{2} - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 4}}{2 \cdot 1} = \frac{5 \pm \sqrt{25 - 16}}{2} = \frac{5 \pm \sqrt{9}}{2} =$
    - $y1 = \frac{8}{2} = 4$
    - $y2 = \frac{2}{2} = 2$
- Ha $y1 = 4 \implies x^{2} = 4 \implies |x| = 2$
    - $x1 = 2$
    - $x2 = -2$
- Ha $y2 = 1 \implies x^{2} = 1 \implies x = 1$
    - $x3 = 1$
    - $x4 = -1$
